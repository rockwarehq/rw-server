# Code Reviews

Deep, adversarially-verified reviews of critical subsystems. Unlike an ADR (which records a decision), a review records **findings at a point in time**: what was checked, what was confirmed with evidence, what was ruled out, and what was deliberately deferred. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Conventions

- Pages live at `apps/docs/src/app/internal/reviews/YYYY-MM-topic/page.mdx`.
- Every finding carries `file:line` evidence and a verdict: **Confirmed**, **Rejected** (checked and disproven — kept so nobody re-litigates it), or **Deferred** (real, deliberately not fixed yet, with a pointer to the tracking issue).
- Line numbers are accurate as of the review's stated commit and will drift — the evidence text is written so the site can be re-found by searching, not just by line.
- When findings are fixed, annotate the finding with the fixing commit/PR rather than deleting it.
