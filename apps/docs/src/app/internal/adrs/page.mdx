# Architecture Decision Records

ADRs capture significant architectural decisions: the context, the decision itself, and its consequences. {{ className: 'lead' }}

They are immutable once accepted — a change of course gets a **new** ADR that supersedes the old one.

## Conventions

- Pages live at `apps/docs/src/app/internal/adrs/NNNN-kebab-case-title/page.mdx` — four digits, zero-padded, monotonically increasing, never reused.
- Statuses: `Proposed` → `Accepted`, and later possibly `Deprecated` or `Superseded by NNNN`.
- To write one: copy [the template](/internal/adrs/template), take the next number, then add it to the table below **and** to `internalNavigation` in `apps/docs/src/components/Navigation.tsx`.
